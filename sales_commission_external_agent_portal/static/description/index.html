Sales Commission External Agent Portal on My Account

Sales Commission Web Portal for Agents and External Partners

This app is allow to show Sales Commission Worksheets and Sales Commission Lines on Website portal to your Commission agents/ Commission Partners.

Main Features:

  • This app allow your commission agents and partners to view Sales Commision Worksheets and commission lines.
  • Search commission worksheet through commission name & commission state.
  • Download Sale Commision Worksheet Report on Commission Worksheet.
  • Sales Commision Lines on My Account Portal for Agents and Partners.
  • Search commission lines through commission line name & commission line state.
  • Alos Available Filter By Commision Worksheet and show its Commision Lines.
  • For more details please watch below screenshots and video.
