Manage CRM with Helpdesk Odoo Apps

CRM Lead Helpdesk Ticket Odoo Apps

Manage CRM with Helpdesk Odoo App helps users to configure CRM helpdesk ticket checkbox. Users can generate a CRM lead directly from a helpdesk ticket vice versa they can generate a helpdesk ticket from the CRM lead.

Features

Easy to Create CRM Lead from Ticket

User can easily create CRM lead directly from helpdesk ticket.

Easy to Create Ticket from CRM Lead

User can easily create helpdesk ticket directly from CRM Lead.
Demo Request Video Tutorial

Configuration for CRM Helpdesk Ticket

Under user configuration you can tick on 'CRM Helpdesk Ticket' checkbox.

Create CRM Lead from Helpdesk Ticket

In helpdesk ticket you can click on "CREATE CRM LEAD" button to create a crm lead directly from the helpdesk ticket.

After created CRM lead you can see 'CRM Lead' smart button on ticket's form view.

Created CRM Lead from Helpdesk Ticket

You can see created CRM lead directly from helpdesk ticket and also view smart button of 'Tickets'.

Create Ticket from CRM Lead

In CRM lead you can click on "CREATE TICKET" button to create a helpdesk ticket directly from the lead.

After created helpdesk ticket you can see 'Tickets' smart button on CRM lead's form view.

Created Helpdesk Ticket from CRM Lead

You can see created helpdesk ticket directly from CRM lead and also view smart button of 'CRM Lead'.
