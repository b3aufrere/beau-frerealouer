Sales Commission on Customer Payment with Product and Category Option

This module provide feature to manage sales commision by Customer Payments with Product and Product Category option. For more details of sales commission module you can see https://apps.odoo.com/apps/modules/15.0/sales_commission_external_user/

This app only add missing feature in sales_commission_external_user to have customer payment with Product and Product Category.

Calculation based on = Product or Product Category

- When we apply commission based on Product and Product Category and want to apply commission based on Payment then it will allow to add Products on Payment form and it will compute commission based on inserted Product Lines on customer Payment screen.
